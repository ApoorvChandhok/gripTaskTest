Help-Age Foundation (Delhi)

Help-Age India is a leading charity platform in India working with and for disadvantaged elderly for more than 2 decades.

About Help-Age

Our Vision

A society where elders are healthy, happy, empowered and socially integrated. We aim to contribute to the building of a society in which people are encouraged and enabled to age productively and with dignity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to work towards developing and implementing activities that enhance productive aging. We will provide services that enable older adults to remain healthy, active and empowered, constantly striving to be a leader in long-term care.

Our Plan

Currently we are projecting over 10 million elderly in India. HelpAge India voices their concerns, so they can lead secure & dignified lives. It works through 19 State Offices across India, runs numerous programs on-ground, addressing elder needs and advocating for their rights.
